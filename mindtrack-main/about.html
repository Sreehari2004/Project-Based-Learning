About MindTrack

MindTrack is dedicated to making mental health assessment accessible and actionable for everyone. Our platform provides a comprehensive evaluation of your mental wellbeing through scientifically-backed questionnaires.

Our Mission

To provide accessible mental health assessment tools that help people understand and improve their mental wellbeing.

Our Approach

We use evidence-based assessment methods and provide personalized insights to help you track and improve your mental health.

Privacy First

Your privacy is our priority. All assessments are confidential and your data is securely protected.

Our Team

MindTrack was created by a team of developers, and designers who are passionate about making mental health assessment more accessible and user-friendly.
